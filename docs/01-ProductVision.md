# Product Vision

## Tagline

**Making AI-powered learning games easy for everyone.**

---

## Why

Teachers and schools want to create engaging and interactive learning experiences for students. However, creating these experiences is often time-consuming and requires multiple disconnected tools.

---

## Who

### Primary Users

- Teachers
- Schools

### Secondary Users

- Students

---

## Problem

Creating interactive learning games is complex and time-consuming. Teachers often need to switch between different platforms and tools to build engaging learning experiences.

---

## Solution

AI-Powered Education is an AI-first platform where teachers can simply describe their ideas and receive a complete interactive learning game generated by AI.

Teachers can review, modify, and customize every part of the generated content before sharing it with their students.

---

## Unique Value Proposition

Making AI-powered learning games easy for everyone.

---

## Product Principles

- AI creates the first draft.
- Teachers stay in control.
- Teachers can modify any AI-generated content.
- Learning games should be simple to create.
- Interactive learning should be accessible to everyone.

---

## Success Criteria

A teacher should be able to create an engaging learning game in minutes instead of hours.

The platform should reduce the time and effort required to create interactive educational experiences.

---

## Long Term Vision

Become the preferred AI-powered platform for teachers and schools to create, customize, and deliver interactive learning games and gamified educational experiences.
